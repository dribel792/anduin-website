Prospectus Roles & Service-Provider Checklist

Every role for the Anduin tokenized-securities programme — hard vs. optional, where it lives (base prospectus vs. final terms), who can fill it, and when it must be ready. Benchmarked against the FMA-approved Ondo Global Markets and Backed / xStocks filings.

Liechtenstein FMA routev1 · 27 Jun 2026Internal — not for indexing
Working checklist — not legal advice. Prepared for internal planning. Every "hard requirement" and every ESMA Category A/B/C classification below must be confirmed by prospectus counsel before the base prospectus is locked. The most common reason a base prospectus is bounced is putting information in final terms that the base did not flag as optional.

The one rule that governs all of this

Under the EU Prospectus Regulation (Reg. 2017/1129, Art. 8) and ESMA's guidelines on final terms, final terms can only fill in blanks the base prospectus explicitly left open (ESMA Category A / B / C fields). You cannot introduce a new party or role in final terms that the base prospectus did not contemplate.

➜ For every multi-party role: park the optionality in the base prospectus ("the Issuer may appoint one or more / additional [role]"), then name the actual party per Series in the final terms. That is what lets you name one now and add more later.

Hedge counterparty

Not namedOPS
Decided
Do not name them. The base discloses hedging risk generically — "the Issuer may enter into back-to-back / hedging arrangements with one or more counterparties." The Bybit account just needs to exist operationally.
Who
Bybit (example) or any exchange / OTC desk where the hedge account is live.
When
Account open before first Series settles; no prospectus deadline.

Paying Agent

Structure-dependentBFT
Decided
Same logic as the Dealer — anchor in the base, allow more via final terms if you even need a classic one. Ondo names no separate paying agent — on-chain settlement + the Administrator/Registrar absorb the function. For sui-generis tokens you can likely collapse this into role 5.

A paying-agent bank, or fold into the Registrar / Administrator.
When

Registrar / Administrator

HardBFT
Decided
This is the role you actually need (not "transfer agent") — Ondo's equivalent of the transfer-agent function. Ondo names The Bank of New York Mellon as Administrator & Registrar.
Who
An institutional administrator / registrar (BNY Mellon-class), or the issuer's appointed registrar.
When
Engaged before base approval.

Transfer Agent

Not needed

N/A — covered by Registrar / Administrator.
When
N/A.

Hard-requirement summary

Must exist before base approval

Issuer (#1), ≥1 Dealer / Manager (#2), Registrar / Administrator (#5), and — if secured — the Security framework (#8).

Per-Series in final terms

Manager, Custodian(s), Security Agent + collateral, Paying Agent (if used).

Operational only — never in the prospectus

Hedge counterparty (#3).

Not needed at all

Transfer Agent (#6).
