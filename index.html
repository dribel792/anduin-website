For TradFi

Built With Banks, Brokers and Exchanges.

Anduin is not a DeFi protocol. It's settlement infrastructure for regulated institutions — designed alongside the firms that will use it.

T+0 Settlement

Atomic on-chain settlement of cash against tokenized assets. No prefunding, no failed trades, no counterparty exposure between trade and settle.

Tokenized Real-World Assets

Issue regulated securities as ERC-3643 tokens — identity-gated, jurisdiction-aware, compliant by construction. Distribute on-chain without rebuilding compliance.

No Balance Sheet Risk

Settlement and counterparty exposure handled on-chain. Your book stays clean. Your custody model stays intact.

TradFi-Grade Controls

Role-based access, insurance fund, oracle-backed pricing, audited contracts. Built to integrate, not to replace.
